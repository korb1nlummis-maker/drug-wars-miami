========================================
   DRUG WARS - Controls & UI Reference
========================================


GAME MODES
----------
- START NEW GAME: Standard 5000-day campaign
- ENDLESS MODE: No day limit, play forever to build your empire
- LOAD GAME: Resume from any of 5 save slots


MAIN INTERFACE
--------------
The game has a sidebar + main content layout:

STATUS BAR (top):
  - Day counter (5000 max or endless)
  - Cash on hand
  - Bank balance / Debt
  - HP, Heat meter, Inventory space, Reputation
  - Investigation level (Clean to Arrest Warrant)
  - Rank (click to view achievements)
  - Territory count, Distribution income

SIDEBAR (left):
  ACTIONS:
    - Travel - Move to a new city
    - Wait - Wait a day (prices shift, events happen)
  SERVICES (varies by city):
    - Bank - Deposit/withdraw cash
    - Loan Shark - Borrow/repay debt
    - Hospital - Heal HP
    - Black Market - Buy weapons, hire crew
    - Stash - Store drugs (Miami only)
  EMPIRE:
    - Crew - Manage hired crew
    - Take Over - Challenge local gang
    - Fronts - Manage front businesses
    - Distribution - Manage drug distribution network
  CHARACTER:
    - Skills - Fallout-style skill tree (4 tabs, 36 skills)
    - Stats - Empire statistics and records
    - Achievements - 240+ achievements across 16 categories
  - Retire - End game early

MAIN CONTENT (right):
  - Current city info, events, drug prices
  - Price sparkline charts
  - Inventory and weapons


TRADING
-------
- Click BUY on any drug row to purchase
- Click SELL on owned drugs to sell
- Prices change every travel/wait
- Watch for EVENT messages - price spikes and crashes
- Territory control: 15% buy discount, 10% sell bonus


TRAVEL & TRANSPORT
------------------
- Click TRAVEL to see destinations grouped by region
- Choose from 17 transport types across 4 tiers:
  Budget: Bus, Car, Motorcycle
  Standard: SUV, Commercial Flight, Speedboat
  Premium: Armored Truck, Go-Fast Boat, Cargo Ship, Private Jet
  Drug Lord: Semi Truck, Narco Sub, Military Convoy, Yacht, Cartel Plane
- Each transport has different cost, speed, risk, and cargo capacity
- Traveling may trigger random events or dialogue encounters


WEAPONS (24 total)
------------------
Available at Black Market locations:
  Melee: Fists, Switchblade, Machete
  Pistols: Saturday Night Special, Beretta, .44 Magnum, Desert Eagle, Gold Deagle
  SMGs: Uzi, MAC-10, MP5
  Shotguns: Sawed-Off, SPAS-12
  Rifles: AK-47, M16, SCAR-H, Galil ACE
  Heavy: RPK, M60, RPG-7, M134 Minigun, M79 Grenade Launcher
  Legendary: Gold-Plated AK-47, "My Little Friend" (M16A1 + M203)


COMBAT
------
When you encounter enemies:
  - FIGHT - Attack (damage based on crew + weapons)
  - RUN - Attempt to flee (may fail, take damage)
  - SURRENDER - Give up (lose drugs/money, may go to court)


CREW MANAGEMENT
---------------
Hire from Black Market (max 4):
  - Street Thug: Cheap fighter, +20 carry
  - Bodyguard: Tank, takes hits for you
  - Smuggler: +50 carry capacity
  - Enforcer: Strong fighter, +15 carry
  - Chemist: Better drug purity (sell prices)
  - Lawyer: Reduces investigation, court bonuses
  - Lookout: Reduces encounter chance
  - Fall Guy: One-use, takes the rap if arrested


SKILL TREE (Fallout-style)
--------------------------
4 categories, 9 skills each (36 total), 3 tiers:

TRAVELLING (blue):
  Tier 1: Road Warrior, Light Packer, Street Smarts
  Tier 2: Smuggler Instinct, Speed Runner, Safe Houses
  Tier 3: Ghost Routes, Logistics Master, Tunnel Network

POWER (red):
  Tier 1: Brawler, Thick Skin, Weapons Expert
  Tier 2: Crew Commander, Body Armor, Headhunter
  Tier 3: Warlord, Bulletproof, Army of One

INFLUENCE (orange):
  Tier 1: Silver Tongue, Master Haggler, Street Rep
  Tier 2: Smooth Talker, The Fence, Informant Network
  Tier 3: Diplomat, Media Mogul, Puppet Master

CONTROL (purple):
  Tier 1: Territory Boss, Supply Chain, Money Man
  Tier 2: Iron Fist, Cartel Network, Laundry King
  Tier 3: Empire Builder, Shadow Government, Kingmaker

PROGRESSION:
  - Earn 2 skill points per Kingpin level up
  - Tier 1: 1 point per rank (no requirements)
  - Tier 2: 2 points per rank (Level 5+, 5 tree ranks)
  - Tier 3: 3 points per rank (Level 10+, 12 tree ranks)
  - Each skill also has prerequisite skills to unlock


DIALOGUE ENCOUNTERS
-------------------
- Random NPCs appear while waiting or travelling
- 15 NPC types, 12 unique encounters
- Multi-choice dialogue with speech skill checks
- Choices affect: cash, drugs, weapons, reputation, heat,
  investigation, buffs, crew, and more
- Build Speech skill through the Influence skill tree


INVESTIGATION & COURT
---------------------
Investigation rises from:
  - Large drug transactions, killing police
  - High heat, disloyal crew snitching

Investigation drops from:
  - Not dealing for a few days
  - Having a Lawyer on crew
  - Laundering money through fronts

If arrested:
  - Pay court contacts to improve odds:
    Dirty Lawyer, Corrupt Judge, Jury Tampering,
    Witness Intimidation, Evidence Tampering, FBI Deal
  - Or use a Fall Guy to skip trial
  - Guilty = prison time + asset forfeiture


FRONT BUSINESSES
----------------
Available at bank locations:
  - Buy businesses for daily income
  - Launder cash (reduces investigation)
  - Types: Taco Stand, Laundromat, Car Wash, Nightclub, etc.


DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM
-------------------
Set up drug distribution in your territories:
  - 6 dealer roles: Corner Dealer, Runner, Trap House Boss,
    Club Connect, Wholesaler, Lieutenant
  - 3 upgrade tiers per location
  - Stock drugs, dealers sell automatically each day
  - Revenue affected by location, drug type, and crew
  - Risk of busts and rival attacks


ACHIEVEMENTS & LEVELING
-----------------------
- 240+ achievements across 16 categories:
  Money, Trading, Travel, Combat, Territory, Crew, Legal,
  Rep, Levels, Skills, Dialogue, Weapons, Business, Buffs,
  Secret (hidden), Endgame
- 20 Kingpin levels from Street Punk to Legendary
- Each level grants gameplay perks + 2 skill points
- Secret achievements unlock by discovery


KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
------------------
- T: Quick travel (from main game screen)
- All other interactions are mouse/touch.
